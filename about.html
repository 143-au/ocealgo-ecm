About Ocealgo

Discovering the power of the ocean for sustainable personal care

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Ocealgo was born from a simple yet powerful idea: to harness the incredible benefits of seaweed for personal care while protecting our planet. Our journey began when our founders discovered the remarkable properties of marine algae during a research expedition along the pristine coastlines of the North Atlantic.

Inspired by the ocean's natural ability to cleanse and regenerate, we developed a revolutionary process to extract the purest seaweed essences, creating wet wipes that are not only effective but also completely biodegradable.

Our Mission

At Ocealgo, we believe that personal care products should work in harmony with nature, not against it. Our mission is to provide families with the highest quality, most sustainable wet wipes on the market while contributing to ocean conservation efforts.
