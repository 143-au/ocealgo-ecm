Baby Wet Wipes

$14.99

Our specially formulated baby wet wipes combine the gentle power of seaweed with the utmost care for your little one's delicate skin. Hypoallergenic and alcohol-free, these wipes provide superior cleaning while maintaining the natural moisture balance of baby's skin.

Key Benefits:

  • Hypoallergenic and alcohol-free
  • Made with gentle seaweed extracts
  • pH balanced for baby's skin
  • Thick, strong, and ultra-soft
  • Dermatologist tested and approved
  • 100% biodegradable

Ingredients:

Purified water, organic seaweed extract (Laminaria digitata), aloe vera, calendula extract, chamomile extract, vitamin E, natural preservatives (no parabens, sulfates, or alcohol).

Usage:

Perfect for diaper changes, cleaning hands, face, and body. Safe for newborns and sensitive skin. Dispose of responsibly - our wipes are fully biodegradable.
